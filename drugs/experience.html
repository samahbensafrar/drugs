Business Icons who overcame addiction :

KHALIL RAFATI, FOUNDER/ OWNER OF SUNLIFE :

Khalil Rafati's life was marked by poverty, abuse, and addiction, leading him to homelessness and multiple struggles with substance abuse. However, he eventually turned his life around through holistic healing modalities like yoga and nutrition. In 2003, he founded SunLife Organics, a successful health food company. Khalil's journey from rock bottom to success and sobriety serves as a powerful reminder of the transformative power of resilience and perseverance.

RYAN BLAIR, ENTREPRENEUR AND AUTHOR:
