DIFFRENT TYPES OF DRUGS AND THEIR EFFECT :

Stimulants:
  • Long-term effects: Cardiovascular problems (e.g., hypertension, heart attacks), addiction, malnutrition, psychosis, mood disorders.
Depressants :
  • Long-term effects: Respiratory depression, addiction, liver damage (with alcohol), cognitive impairment, increased risk of accidents and injuries.
Hallucinogens:
  • Long-term effects: Flashbacks (recurrences of drug experiences without taking the drug), persistent psychosis, increased risk of accidents and injuries during intoxication.
Cannabis:
  • Long-term effects: Cognitive impairment (especially in adolescents), respiratory issues (from smoking), addiction (in some users), increased risk of mental health disorders (especially in vulnerable individuals), decreased motivation and productivity.
