PRESARVATIO AND RECOVERY

some strategies to help individuals stay away from drugs:
  • Educate Yourself: Learn about the dangers and consequences of drug use.
  • Surround Yourself with Positive Influences: Positive peer pressure can be a powerful deterrent.
  • Avoid Triggering Environments: Stay away from places or situations where drugs are readily available or commonly used.
tips that might help overcome addiction:
  • Seek Professional Help: Consider reaching out to a therapist, counselor, or support group specialized in addiction recovery.
  • Develop Coping Mechanisms: Find healthy ways to cope with stress, anxiety, or negative emotions that don't involve substance use.
  • Build a Support System: Surround yourself with people who understand your challenges and are supportive of your recovery efforts.
